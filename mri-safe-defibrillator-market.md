<p><p>The MRI Safe Defibrillator market is categorized into two primary types: Single Chamber MRI Safe Defibrillators and Double Chamber MRI Safe Defibrillators. Single Chamber devices are designed to deliver shocks to one heart chamber, making them simpler and generally smaller, suitable for specific patient conditions. In contrast, Double Chamber MRI Safe Defibrillators can pace and shock both the atrium and ventricle, offering more comprehensive cardiac support, particularly beneficial for patients with complex arrhythmias, ensuring safety during MRI procedures.</p></p>
