<p><ul><li>FP-QCL</li><li>DFB-QCL</li><li>ECqcl</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mid-IR Quantum Cascade Laser (QCL) market is categorized into three primary types: FP-QCL, DFB-QCL, and EC-QCL. FP-QCL (Fabry-Pérot QCL) offers broad emission and is suitable for sensing applications. DFB-QCL (Distributed Feedback QCL) ensures single-mode operation and enhanced wavelength stability, making it ideal for telecommunications and precision measurements. EC-QCL (External Cavity QCL) features tunability across a wide spectral range, addressing applications in spectroscopy and environmental monitoring. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>
