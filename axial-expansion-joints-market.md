<p><p>The axial expansion joints market is categorized primarily into flange type and welding type. Flange type expansion joints feature flanged ends for easy installation and replacement, making them suitable for various industrial applications where flexibility and reliability are required. Welding type expansion joints are integrally welded to the piping system, providing a more permanent solution that can handle higher pressures and temperatures. Both types enable effective absorption of thermal expansion and contraction in piping systems, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
