<p><ul><li>Vane Air Motor</li><li>Piston Air Motor</li><li>Gear Air Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air motors market consists of three main types: vane air motors, piston air motors, and gear air motors. Vane air motors operate with sliding vanes and are known for their compact size and efficiency. Piston air motors utilize reciprocating pistons, offering high torque and durability for heavy-duty applications. Gear air motors use gears to convert compressed air into mechanical power, providing consistent speed and torque control. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, driving growth across various sectors.</p></p>
