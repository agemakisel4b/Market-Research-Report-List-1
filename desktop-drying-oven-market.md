<p><p>The Desktop Drying Oven market encompasses two primary types: heat drying and air drying. Heat drying utilizes controlled temperatures to evaporate moisture efficiently, making it suitable for materials requiring rapid drying. In contrast, air drying relies on ambient airflow to remove moisture gradually, ideal for more delicate items or those sensitive to high temperatures. Both methods cater to diverse applications in laboratories, research, and industrial settings, providing customizable solutions for effective drying processes tailored to specific material requirements.</p></p>
