<p><p>The steel alloys market is categorized into three primary types: binary alloys, ternary alloys, and multielement alloys. Binary alloys consist of two elements, typically providing basic improvements in strength and corrosion resistance. Ternary alloys introduce a third element, enhancing properties like toughness and workability. Multielement alloys are complex mixtures with multiple elements, designed to achieve specific traits such as high-performance in extreme conditions. Each type caters to diverse industrial applications, shaping the landscape of modern materials.</p></p>
