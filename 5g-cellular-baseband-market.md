<p><p>The 5G cellular baseband market is categorized into Single-Core and Multi-Core cellular basebands. Single-Core cellular basebands utilize a single processing unit, ideal for basic applications and cost-sensitive devices. In contrast, Multi-Core cellular basebands incorporate multiple processing units, allowing for enhanced performance, lower latency, and better multitasking. This makes them suitable for advanced applications, such as augmented reality and IoT. Overall, the choice between these types depends on the performance requirements and application demands of the device.</p></p>
