Speed and distance sensors, including LiDAR and radar, measure distance accurately. Position sensors like GPS and GNSS provide location data. Pressure sensors monitor altitude, while current sensors track energy usage. Ultrasonic, light, and altimeter sensors contribute to obstacle detection and operational efficiency in aerial applications.</p></p>
