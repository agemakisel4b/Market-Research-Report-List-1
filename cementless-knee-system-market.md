<p><p>The cementless knee system market encompasses various components, including the femoral system, tibial system, and hip system. The femoral system focuses on the fixation of the femur during knee replacement procedures, promoting natural bone integration. The tibial system addresses the stabilization of the tibia, often utilizing porous designs for enhanced bone growth. The hip system involves similar cementless techniques for hip replacements, allowing for better biological fixation and reducing reliance on cement, which can lead to improved longevity of implants.</p></p>
