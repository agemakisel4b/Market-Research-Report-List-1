<p><ul><li>Bench-top</li><li>Transcutaneous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bilirubin meters market is categorized into two main types: bench-top and transcutaneous. Bench-top bilirubin meters are laboratory devices used for precise measurement of bilirubin levels in blood samples, often employed in clinical settings for accurate diagnostics. Conversely, transcutaneous bilirubin meters are non-invasive devices that estimate bilirubin levels by measuring light absorption through the skin, offering quick and convenient screenings, particularly in neonates. Both types are integral for effective management and monitoring of jaundice and related conditions.</p></p>
