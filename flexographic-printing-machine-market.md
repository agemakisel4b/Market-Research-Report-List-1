<p><ul><li>Unit -type Machine</li><li>Central Impression Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexographic printing machine market consists of two main types: unit-type machines and central impression machines. Unit-type machines feature individual printing stations, allowing for modular setups that can be customized based on production needs. Conversely, central impression machines have a single large cylindrical impression drum, providing consistent pressure and high-quality prints across larger materials. Each type caters to distinct production requirements, with unit-type machines offering flexibility and versatility, while central impression machines excel in speed and uniformity for high-volume printing processes.</p></p>
