<p><ul><li>Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors</li><li>Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Magnetic Sensors market is divided into two main types: Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors and Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors. Linear 3D Magnetic Sensors measure magnetic fields along three axes, providing precise linear position data, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, Rotary 3D Magnetic Sensors detect rotational movement around multiple axes, making them ideal for applications like robotics and electronic devices where orientation is crucial. Both types enhance automation and improve accuracy in various technologically advanced systems.</p></p>
