<p><p>The forklift truck tire market comprises three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic forklift tires are air-filled, offering excellent shock absorption and traction, ideal for outdoor use. Solid forklift tires are made from durable rubber, providing puncture resistance and longer lifespan, suitable for heavy-duty indoor applications. Polyurethane forklift tires combine lightweight properties with high resilience, making them perfect for smooth surfaces and frequent maneuvering, often used in warehouses and distribution centers where noise reduction is essential.</p></p>
