<p><p>Electron Beam Machining (EBM) encompasses various applications including welding, surface treatment, and drilling. In welding, high-energy electron beams fuse materials with precision, ensuring strong joints without filler materials. Surface treatment uses EBM to modify material properties, enhancing hardness or corrosion resistance through controlled melting and cooling. Drilling involves creating small, precise holes in hard-to-machine materials, making EBM ideal for components in aerospace and automotive industries. This versatility drives demand across sectors, highlighting its importance in advanced manufacturing.</p></p>
