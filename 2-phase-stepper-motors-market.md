<p><strong>The 2 Phase Stepper Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Type</li><li>Unipolar Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The two-phase stepper motor market is divided into bipolar and unipolar types. Bipolar stepper motors utilize two winding coils, allowing for better torque and control, commonly used in applications requiring high precision. In contrast, unipolar stepper motors have a simpler design with one winding per phase, offering easier control and lower cost, making them suitable for lighter applications. Each type serves distinct market needs, influencing their adoption across various industries, such as robotics, automation, and CNC machinery.</p></p>
