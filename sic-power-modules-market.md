<p><strong>The SiC Power Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hybrid SiC Modules</li><li>Full SiC Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SiC power modules market consists of two main types: Hybrid SiC Modules and Full SiC Modules. Hybrid SiC Modules combine silicon-based components with silicon carbide for improved performance in specific applications, offering cost-effective solutions while enhancing efficiency and thermal management. In contrast, Full SiC Modules utilize only silicon carbide materials, providing superior power density, efficiency, and thermal performance. This makes Full SiC Modules ideal for high-performance applications such as electric vehicles and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
