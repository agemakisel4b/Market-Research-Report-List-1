<p><p>The Farm Animal Anti-Infective Medicine Market is segmented into two key types: external use and internal use. External use products include antibiotics, antiseptics, and disinfectants applied topically to treat infections on the skin or prevent microbial growth in facilities. Internal use medications involve oral or injectable treatments that target infections within animals' bodies, addressing diseases systemically. Both categories are essential for maintaining animal health, ensuring efficient livestock production, and preventing the spread of infections in agricultural settings.</p></p>
